Movie Quote

I guess it comes down a simple choice: Get busy living, or get busy dying. Source

Plot

Tim Robbins plays as Andy Dufresne, a former successful banker who was convicted of murder and sent to the Shawshank Prison. Andy meets a guy named Red, and becomes one of his good friends. Throughout the movie, Andy helps the Warden and the other guards with income tax returns and other financial advice. Towards the end Andy, takes the Wardens money, and escapes prison through his cell, after digging up a tunnel behind a poster in his cell.
